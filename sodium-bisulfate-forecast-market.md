Sodium Bisulfate, with the molecular formula NaHSO4, is an inorganic, white to yellow powder. It is a sodium salt of bisulfate ion that is manufactured by neutralization of sulfuric acid by sodium chloride or sodium hydroxide. It is an acid kind of salt which is formed by partial neutralisation of sulfuric acid by an equivalent of sodium base. It is typically in the form of either sodium hydroxide (lye) or sodium chloride.
